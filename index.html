FAQ

Q: What documents are required to book a flat? +

You'll need identity proof (Passport/Aadhar), address proof, PAN card, and passport-size photographs. For NRIs, additional documents like overseas address proof and passport copy are required.

Q: Can I book a flat online? +

Yes, we offer a seamless online booking process. You can schedule a virtual site visit, review documents, and complete the booking formalities remotely.

Q: Is the property RERA registered? +

Yes, all our projects are RERA registered and comply with all regulatory requirements.

Q: What is the booking amount and is it refundable? +

The booking amount varies by project. It is refundable as per RERA guidelines if the project doesn't meet specified timelines.

Q: Will you help with documentation and registration? +

Absolutely! Our dedicated team assists with all documentation, registration, and legal formalities to ensure a smooth process.
